Comprehensive Compliance Framework

Our systematic approach to safety ensures regulatory compliance across all markets

Failure Mode & Effects Analysis (FMEA)

Systematic evaluation of potential failure modes and their effects on system operation, with risk priority number assessment and mitigation strategies.

Hazard & Operability Study (HAZOP)

Structured examination of processes and systems to identify and evaluate problems that may represent risks to personnel or equipment.

Risk Assessment Matrix

Quantitative risk assessment combining likelihood and severity to prioritize safety improvements and control measures.

Thermal Testing

Comprehensive thermal cycling, thermal shock, and elevated temperature testing to ensure battery safety under extreme conditions.

Mechanical Testing

Vibration, shock, crush, and penetration testing to validate physical integrity and crashworthiness of battery enclosures.

Electrical Testing

Overcharge, short circuit, and insulation resistance testing to verify electrical safety and protection systems.

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of voltage, current, temperature, and isolation resistance with automated alerting and logging.

Data Logging & Analysis

Comprehensive data collection for performance tracking, predictive maintenance, and continuous improvement.

Audit Trail

Complete traceability of all safety-critical operations, maintenance activities, and system events for regulatory compliance.
