Our Journey

Engineering Sustainable Mobility

At PEV Labs, we're not just building technology—we're building a cleaner future. Through innovative engineering and sustainable solutions, we're transforming transportation one vehicle at a time.

Our Evolution

2018

The Beginning

Founded by engineers passionate about sustainable transportation. Started with our first EV conversion lab in Accra, Ghana.

2019

First Major Project

Successfully converted a fleet of 15 delivery vehicles to electric, proving the viability of commercial EV conversions.

2021

Expansion & Innovation

Opened offices in North America and Europe. Developed proprietary battery management systems and charging solutions.

2023

Industry Recognition

Awarded "Most Innovative Clean Tech Company" by Sustainable Mobility Association. Reached 200+ successful projects.

2025

Future Vision

Launching next-generation modular EV platforms and expanding grid-scale energy storage solutions across Africa.
