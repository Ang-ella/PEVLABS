Strategic Alliance

Powering the Future of EV Technology

ProDrive EV-LABS partners with Automotive Engineering & Technical Services (AETS) to expand diagnostics and battery capabilities, driving innovation through collaborative research and development.

Collaborative Excellence in EV Innovation

Our strategic partnership combines the technical expertise of PEV-LABS with the engineering capabilities of AETS to deliver comprehensive EV solutions.

Together we conduct R&D on safe cell replacement, modular pack redesign and custom battery manufacturing. This collaboration provides access to a high-voltage laboratory, skilled technicians and heavy equipment, enabling larger-scale conversions and future plans for a dedicated conversion garage.

The synergy between our organizations creates a powerful innovation engine for the electric vehicle industry, addressing complex challenges with integrated solutions.
